Code of Conduct

The Scavengers Network Indie Podcast Showcase (SNIPS) is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or technology choices.

We do not tolerate harassment of guests or attendees in any form. Abusive language and behavior is not appropriate for any SNIPS programming, including live shows, Twitter and other online media. SNIPS participants violating these rules may be expelled from the event at the discretion of the SNIPS organisers.

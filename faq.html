FAQ

When is the Scavengers Network Indie Podcast Showcase (SNIPS) and where can I find it?

SNIPS is a virtual event that will take place Friday, May 21st to Sunday, May 23rd. It will be streamed live on Twitch, Facebook, and here!

Do you have a code of conduct?

Yes, and you can find it here.

Do you need a ticket?

No, this is a completely free event!

Are there any age restrictions?

While a lot of the shows will be family-friendly, some shows fall under the explicit rating on iTunes. These shows will be flagged as such on the schedule and attendees are responsible for choosing age appropriate content to view.

I won't be able to attend, but I want to support the event. What can I do?

Tell all of your friends about SNIPS and follow The Scavengers Network on Twitter and Instagram! We also have a Patreon where you can access bonus content for as little as $2 a month.

Can I sponsor SNIPS 2021?

If you are interested in sponsorship opportunities, please contact us at sponsorships@snipshowcase.com

I have a question that was not answered here

If you have any questions that were not answered here, please send an email to inquiries@snipshowcase.com
